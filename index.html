About Me

Welcome to my Epicodus project portfolio. My name is Peter Grimm. The purpose of this page show off some of the projects I've been working on over the weeks at this program. Additionally, it's a good place to tell you a little more about myself.

I grew up in the suburbs near Bellevue, WA and I was interested in computers from an early age. Some of my earliest memories consist of working with my dad to try and manage system memory to get games to work on an old 386-based PC, and I've been hooked ever since. My fourth grade class undertook a year-long project of creating computer games written in in the language Logo. Mine was called Danger Zone and featured aeronautical trivia and interactive segments where you would have to navigate planes to their landing strips.
